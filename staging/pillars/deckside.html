DeckSide (RECORD)

Catch verification and certification

Overview

DeckSide provides the "RECORD" checkpoint where catch data is validated, certified, and permanently recorded on the blockchain for tamper-evident traceability.

Key Features:

  • GPS-verified catch recording
  • Observer validation
  • QR code generation for traceability
  • Species identification and weight validation
  • Certification attachment (MSC, BAP, etc.)
  • Quota compliance checking

Use Cases

For Fishing Vessels:

  • Record catch data immediately upon landing
  • Generate QR codes for each catch lot
  • Attach observer validation and signatures

For Processors:

  • Scan QR codes to verify catch authenticity
  • View full catch history and certifications
  • Maintain chain of custody documentation

For Consumers:

  • Scan QR code on product packaging
  • View vessel, location, and certification data
  • Verify sustainable sourcing claims
